## Usage Guidelines (Swapped for Light Theme)

### 1. Main Background / Body Background
- **Color:** Pink Lavender (`#DFBBCA`)
- **Usage:** Use as the primary background color for the entire website to create a warm, gentle atmosphere.

### 2. Header and Footer
- **Color:** Pink Lavender (`#DFBBCA`) or White (`#FFFFFF`) with borders.
- **Usage:** Harmonize with the main background.

### 3. Content Areas / Story Cards / Article Sections
- **Color:** White (`#FFFFFF`) or White with low opacity (`rgba(255,255,255,0.5)`)
- **Usage:** Background for readable content areas to ensure high contrast against the Pink Lavender background.

### 4. Main Headings (H1, H2)
- **Color:** Wine Red (`#A30E2B`) or Vampire Black (`#0E0204`)
- **Usage:** Use dark, rich colors for titles to make them pop against the light background.

### 5. Body Text / Paragraph Text
- **Color:** Vampire Black (`#0E0204`)
- **Usage:** High contrast text for readability.

### 6. Primary Action Buttons (CTA)
- **Color:** Wine Red (`#A30E2B`)
- **Text:** White (`#FFFFFF`) or Pink Lavender (`#DFBBCA`)

### 7. Secondary Buttons / Less Important Actions
- **Color:** Old Lavender (`#7C5D72`)

### 8. Links and Subheadings
- **Color:** Twilight Lavender (`#774972`)
